Harrisonburg, Virginia

Experience the Friendly City

Affenctionately known as the friendly city, Harrisonburg is home to countless restaurants, shops, and more. It is also home to the beautiful campus of James Madison University. This tight-knit and bustling community is located in the heart of the Shenandoah Valley, and has countless opportunities for hiking, camping, and other outdoor recreation.

Visit Harrisonburg, Virginia to experience unlimited possibilities and see all that the Shenandoah Valley has to offer!
